Aptitude Test

1. 5005-5000/10

2. After getting two successive discounts, a shirt with a list price of Rs.150 is available at Rs.105. If the second discount is 12.5%, find the first discount.

3. I walk a certain distance and ride back taking a total time of 37 minutes. I could walk both ways in 55 minutes. How long would it take me to ride both ways?

4. The value of machines depriciates at the rate of 10% per annum. If its present value is 1,62,000, what will be its worth after two years? What was the value of the machine two years ago?

5. A, B and C enters into patnership. A invest 3 times as much as B invests and B invests two-third of what C invests. At the end of the year the profit earned is Rs.6600. What is the share of the B?

6. A is twice as good workman as B and together they finish a peice of work in 18 days. In how many days A alone finish the work?

7. A train is 150m long is running with a speed of 68kmph. In what time will it pass a man who is running at a speed of 8kmph in same direction in which train is going?

8. Find the SI on Rs.68,000 at 2/3% per annum for 9 months.

Answers

1. 5005-5000/10
=5005-500=4505

2. Let the first discount be x%
Then, 87.5% of (100-x)% of 150 = 105
87.5/100 * (100-x)/100 * 150 = 105
-> (100-x) = 105 * 100 * 100/150 * 87.5 = 80

3. Let the distance be x km. then,
(time taken to walk x km) + (time taken to ride x km) = 37 min
(time taken to walk 2x km) + (time taken to ride 2x km) = 74min
But,time taken to walk 2x km = 55 min
Time taken to ride 2x km = (74 - 55)min = 19 min

4. Value of the machine after 2 years
= Rs.[162000*(1-10/100)2 = Rs.(162000*9/10*9/10) = Rs.131220.
Value of the machine two years ago
= Rs.[162000/(1-10/100)2] = Rs.(162000*10/9*10/9) = Rs.200000

5. Let C's capital = Rs.x Then, B's capital be 2/3x
A's capital = Rs. (3 * 2/3x) = Rs. 2x.
Ratio of their capitals = 2x:2/3x:x = 6:2:3
Hence,B's share = Rs.(6600*2/11) = 1200

6. (A's 1 day's work) : (B's 1 day's work) = 2:1
(A + B)'s 1 day's work = 1/18
A's 1 day's work = (1/18 * 2/3) = 1/27
Hence A alone can finish the work in 27 days.

7. Speed of the train relative to man = (68 - 8)kmph = (60 * 5/18)m/sec = (50/3)m/sec
Time taken by thetrain to cross the man
= Time taken by it to cover 150m at (50/3)m/sec = (150 * 3/50)sec = 9 sec.

P = 68,000 R = 2/3% T = 9/12 years = 3/4 years
S.I. = Rs.(68,000 * 2/3 * 3/4 * 1/100) = 340
